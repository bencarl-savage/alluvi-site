Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Who We Are

Alluvi Healthcare UK operates the website buyalluvi.co.uk. We are a UK-based research peptide supplier with operations in London and Northern Ireland. Contact: support@buyalluvi.co.uk | +44 7394 241184.

2. What Data We Collect

When you contact us or place an order, we may collect:

  • Name and contact details (WhatsApp number or email address)
  • Delivery address for order fulfilment
  • Order history and communication records
  • Website usage data via anonymous analytics (if applicable)

We do not collect payment card details — payments are processed through third-party payment providers.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your data to:

  • Process and fulfil your research orders
  • Communicate order status and delivery tracking
  • Respond to support and technical queries
  • Maintain records required by UK law

We do not use your data for marketing without explicit consent. We do not sell your data to third parties.

4. WhatsApp Communications

When you contact us via WhatsApp, your messages are processed through WhatsApp's platform (Meta Inc.). Please refer to WhatsApp's privacy policy for information on how they handle your data. We retain WhatsApp order communications for order fulfilment and dispute resolution purposes.

5. Data Retention

We retain order and communication records for a period of 3 years in accordance with UK business record-keeping requirements. After this period, data is securely deleted.

6. Your Rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data (subject to legal obligations)
  • Object to processing of your data
  • Complain to the ICO (ico.org.uk) if you believe your rights have been violated

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at support@buyalluvi.co.uk.

7. Cookies

Our website uses minimal cookies for basic functionality. We do not use tracking cookies or third-party advertising cookies. No cookie consent banner is required for strictly necessary cookies.

8. Security

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your data. Our website is served over HTTPS (SSL encrypted). We do not store payment data.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The current version will always be available at buyalluvi.co.uk/privacy.html with the date of last update.

10. Contact

For any privacy-related queries, contact us at support@buyalluvi.co.uk or via WhatsApp at +44 7394 241184.
